What is Cyberpunk 2077?

Cyberpunk 2077 is a videogame that gained massive popularity due to its immersive, futuristic world. With high-quality graphics and a complex storyline, the game allows players to experience life in a society influenced by advanced technology and cybernetics. This game includes deep gameplay and character customization, providing players with a truly unique experience.

The Game Studio behind Cyberpunk 2077

Cyberpunk 2077 was developed by CD Projekt Red, a renowned Polish game development studio known for creating rich, immersive worlds. CD Projekt Red gained global acclaim for The Witcher series, particularly The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt. The studio is celebrated for its dedication to storytelling, player choice, and high-quality game design, which all contributed to the success of Cyberpunk 2077.

Phantom Liberty DLC

Phantom Liberty is an exciting expansion for Cyberpunk 2077, introducing new missions, characters, and areas to explore within the dark, neon-lit streets of Night City. Players get to delve deeper into the game's rich lore, exploring a high-stakes espionage storyline that adds fresh excitement to the Cyberpunk universe.
